Mental Health Awareness

Mental health matters. Learn about emotional well-being, support systems, coping strategies, and healthy living habits.

What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to emotional, psychological, and social well-being.

  • Cope with everyday stress
  • Function in school or work
  • Maintain healthy relationships
  • Recover from setbacks
  • Experience balanced emotions

Factors Affecting Mental Health

Biological Factors

  • Genetics
  • Hormones
  • Poor sleep

Psychological Factors

  • Stress
  • Trauma
  • Negative thinking

Social Factors

  • Bullying
  • Loneliness
  • Work pressure

Signs of Mental Health Issues

Emotional Signs

  • Sadness
  • Anxiety
  • Mood swings

Behavioral Signs

  • Withdrawal
  • Loss of interest
  • Difficulty concentrating

Physical Signs

  • Tiredness
  • Sleep changes
  • Headaches

Healthy Living Tips

Eat Healthy

Eat fruits, vegetables, and drink enough water.

Exercise

Exercise daily to reduce stress and boost mood.

Sleep Well

Get 7–9 hours of sleep every night.

Stay Connected

Spend time with supportive friends and family.
